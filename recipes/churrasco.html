Churrasco

Skirt Steak AKA Churrasco

The perfect homemade steak that is tender, juicy, and savory. Simple to prepare, simple to cook, and all within the comfort of your own home.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of trimmed churrasco
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon of white whine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of extera virgin olive oil
  • Vegtable oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Rub.Rub a generous amount of salt and pepper into both sides of the steaks.
  2. Rub. Rub the onion powder into the steaks, dividing it evenly.
  3. Put.Put the steaks into a baking dish or container that holds them comfortably.
  4. Sprinkle.Sprinkle the vinegar over the steaks and brush them with the olive oil.
  5. Marinate.Marinate the steaks at room temperature for up to 30 minutes or refrigerate for up to 2 days.
  6. Heat.Heat a gas grill to medium-high or heat a large grill pan over medium-high heat.
  7. Grill.Grill the steaks, turning only once, to desired doneness.
  8. Remove.Remove from the grill and let rest 5 minutes.
